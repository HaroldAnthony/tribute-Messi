Why this site exists?

July 21, 2024 by Harold Anthony

The whole purpose behind the creation of this heavily-bootstrapped webpage, and the crazy mind behind all this glazing.

SHORT STORY - I'm a college graduate, looking for an excuse to sharpen my Bootstrap skills (just effective copy-pasting).

LONG STORY - I've been crazy about football since 2011, and being an ardent follower of FC Barcelona sucks, atleast in 2024. What sucks even more is the fact that I'm not able to land a job, maybe because it is 2024. But hey, my favourite footballer just completed a three-peat of major international trophies, so it's not that bad. But again, it's not like he's aging backward, and he might retire before the potential Finalissima clash with Boy Wonder Lamine Yamal and the La Roja.

The Blaugrana just aren't the same without their all-time top scorer, who left amidst the club's financial trouble (and apparently, because of it!).

This wannabe webpage is going to be all about THAT sportsperson, and why I've been amazed by watching his magic unfold (no pun intended).
